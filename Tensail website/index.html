About


Tensail's mission is to eliminate inneficient tensile structures and unleash the technology's full potential

To do this we've filed a patent for a method of building these structures with radical improvements. We've reduced the number of people necessary to build, improved the time taken to build and reduced weight of the structures, further resulting in reduced CO2 during transport
